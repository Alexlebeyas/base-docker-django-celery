Create droplet

Once logged in to your account, aim for the top right green button "Create Droplet". Here is our most common settings for a VPS.

  1. OS: Ubuntu 14.04
  2. Size: 5$/month
  3. Region: Toronto or New York, any datacenter
  4. Additional options: Backups
  5. Hostname: Usually the project name

Add your SSH keys can click Create. Digital ocean will redirect your to the dashboard and give you an IP for your new droplet.
